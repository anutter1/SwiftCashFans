SwiftNode Information

With SwiftCash, collaterals for SwiftNodes will require 50K SWIFT. SwiftNodes receive appx. 20% of the minimum inflation or 2% of the maximum inflation. Each SwiftNode will be able to cast one vote for or against a proposal, so the minimum stake required to have a say in proposals is 50K SWIFT. You can set up a node on Dextro and buy your collateral from Crex 24 and AltMarkets.
